Call for Demos

Deep learning IndabaX Morocco 2019 invites the submission of Demos. The goal is, first, to provide a good platform for exchanging ideas between theory and practice; second, to showcase the applicability of different AI technologies to real-world problems and third, to obtain feedback from experts and users from academia and industry.

Demos could be research prototypes or operational systems built using AI technologies in general or deep learning in particular to solve real world problems in society or economy (Education, health, agriculture, security, environment, industry, etc.)

Whether you are an engineering school graduate or still a MSc or Eng. student , this call is for you. IndabaX Morocco gives you an opportunity to expose your innovative ideas and prototypes to your peers and eventual partners from academia and industry.

An AWARD will be granted to the most impressive demo by the program committee.

If you want to participate you just have to follow these rules for competition:

  • Eligibility: Open for all participants including undergraduate students (Engineering, Bachelor, Master students, etc.)
  • Submission: Demo description and, optionally, a link to your demo video and/or a PDF document containing details of your demo.
